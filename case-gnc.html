GNC India

GNC

GNC is a leading global specialty retailer of health and wellness products that takes pride to be the world's largest company of its kind devoted exclusively to help its customers improve the quality of their lives.

GNC approached us to design a campaign to launch its official stores in India in partnership with Guardian Nutrition India.

The fitness and supplemental nutrition category in India has exploded in the last decade with the growing gym culture. But this segment although mature, came with stiff competition and aggressive pricing to be a good entry point for a new premium brand. To our advantage, GNCs product range was not restricted to proteins and muscle building products alone. It's assortment was a diverse mix of protein, performance supplements, weight management supplements, vitamins, herbs and greens and wellness supplements. So we proposed to take a different approach, and targeted the lifestyle and health market, instead of the more obvious fitness and built a story about Living Well.
